Q: When is a security review mandatory?
A: A security review is mandatory for any new application deployment, onboarding a technology vendor, major architectural changes, or whenever sensitive data assets are impacted.

Q: What documents should teams provide?
A: The minimum artifacts include: architecture diagrams, data flow maps, role-matrix, and security control checklist.

Q: Who owns remediation tasks?
A: Responsibility sits with the product or platform owner, but timelines are monitored by the Security Compliance function.

Q: How often are controls monitored?
A: All approved controls are reviewed every quarter, unless otherwise mandated by regulatory bodies.

Q: What if our team cannot meet the remediation deadline?
A: Exceptions must be escalated to the CISO with business justification.
